How to improve sleep quality

  • Avoid using your phone or screens 30–60 minutes before bed.
  • Keep your bedroom cool, dark, and quiet.
  • Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, even on weekends.
  • Use your bed only for sleep and relaxation, not for work or scrolling.
  • Limit caffeine in the afternoon and evening.

Fall asleep faster

  • Try 4‑7‑8 breathing: inhale 4 seconds, hold 7, exhale 8.
  • Do a quick body scan: relax each part of your body from toes to head.
  • Write down worries or to‑dos before bed so your mind can let go.
  • Keep lights dim in the hour before sleep.

Fun sleep facts

  • Most vivid dreams happen during REM sleep.
  • Some people dream in black and white.
  • Good sleep helps memory, mood, and creativity.
  • Humans spend about a third of their lives sleeping.
